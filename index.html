Professional Biography

A career built on integrity,
governance & impact

"Law is not merely a set of rules — it is the architecture within which organisations build trust, operate with integrity, and create sustainable value."

Bhavik Desai is an international legal, compliance, governance, and investigations executive with more than 22 years of experience advising multinational healthcare, pharmaceutical, and life sciences organisations across the Middle East, Africa, India, Turkey, Israel, Eastern Europe, and Latin America.

During a 14-year leadership career with Bristol-Myers Squibb, he served as Senior Director and Head of Legal & Compliance for international markets — partnering with executive leadership on corporate governance, market access, regulatory strategy, investigations, complex transactions, public policy, intellectual property protection, crisis management, and enterprise risk.

His career began in Mumbai, where he trained simultaneously across corporate law, company secretarial practice, and commercial litigation — building a multi-disciplinary foundation across listed-company governance, SEBI filings, and courtroom representation. At Wockhardt Ltd, he moved into complex transactional work — cross-border M&A, IPO preparation, and debt restructuring. Joining MSD (Fulford India Ltd) as Company Secretary and Legal Head deepened his experience in multinational pharmaceutical law, and he played a central role in the operational integration of Schering-Plough, Organon, and MSD businesses in India.

Throughout his career, Bhavik has supported major cross-border acquisitions, integrations, and divestitures, led high-profile investigations and dispute resolution matters, and advised on innovative healthcare access initiatives, privacy, data governance, localisation, and digital transformation programmes.

He holds dual Bachelor degrees in Commerce and Law (registered legal practitioner), dual Company Secretary qualifications across India and the UK, a PGDBA in Finance, WIPO Advanced IP certifications with distinction, and a Diploma in Cyber Law.

Today, he advises organisations as an independent consultant on governance, compliance transformation, investigations, data privacy, regulatory risk, and strategic growth initiatives across emerging markets.
